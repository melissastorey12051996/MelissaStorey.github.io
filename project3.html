2024 Womens Activism Calendar

Tasked to create a calendar based around activism with an added challenge of working as a team with first year students, my group decided to focus our topic on womens issues. Our selected issue range from when women got the right to vote all the way to current hot topics of reproductive rights. In addition, the perameters of the calendar was to use expiremental typography and limit the illustrative elements surrounding our choice of words. My favourite design pieces are the ones I can make a personal connection or be a platform for others that do not have the ability to start the conversation.
