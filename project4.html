One Roof

One Roof is a retirement trade show that prioritizes keeping you in your home. The logo and trade show was designed in partnership with Julia Boss-Zister and Nicholas Cesarini. Our name is representative of our goal: everyone should age with dignity in the space that they created their life in. This inspired our tagline “Your home. Your way.” Our logo is crafted from the concept of two windows overlapping and interlocked, helping symbolize unity and togetherness in your place of comfort. A bold, mono-weight, sans-serif typeface is utilized alongside the logo allowing accessibility and comfort for our target audience.
