Miss Mary's Jam

Miss Mary’s is a fictional jam company that specializes in spicy jams. When designing the illustrations for the jam I knew I wanted to incorporate human characters, creating them in a manner that was more adult focused then for adolescents. Flat art works well with the chosen target audience and having the characters sit on the peppers adds a level of cheeky fun! With further expansion of new flavours within Miss Mary’s the template has already been established, which would provide effortless branding amongst other products.
