Hello, I'm Melissa!

Currently you can find me finishing my second year at Conestoga College’s Graphic Design program in Kitchener, Ontario. I am excited to be going into my third and final year in the fall of 2023!

Throughout my time at Conestoga College I have been fortunate to explore many avenues of graphic design, which have lead me to find my passion in identity creation, product design, and editorials. Each allowing me to connect my interest illustration and photography with graphic design.

In 2016 I graduated from the Photography program at Fanshawe College. Having the ability to combine my passion for photography and good design has allowed me to find the best of both worlds. I look forward to future projects that allow me to embrace the full scope of my creative capabilites while exploring and pushing boundaries further.

My favourite and most rewarding projects are the ones where I can use graphic design to amplify the voices for groups of people that feel like they are unwanted and forgotten. While challenging, my most recent project of creating the 2024 Women's Activism Calendar has been extremely gratifying to see something I am passionate about come together and shed light on so many important issues still facing women today. While graphic design cannot fix all the injustices in the world, it is a great tool to help spread awarness and bring forward these topics that are easy to shy away from.
