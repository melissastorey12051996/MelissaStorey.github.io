Pairing Book

For our paring book assignment we were tasked to pair instructions with object of our choice. I paired alcoholic recipes with movie monsters! Through this book creation I was able blend my love of scary movies with alcoholic beverages that sounded appealing by personal preferences. All imagery, aside from movie still that were used for double page spreads, were photographed and edited by me.
